Why compare solar + battery together?

Solar produces during the day. Batteries can store energy for evening use, backup needs, and participation in eligible battery programs.

☀

Daytime production

Solar can offset daytime usage and help produce energy when Sacramento sun is strongest.

◐

Evening use

A battery may store excess solar energy for later use when household demand often rises.

Backup needs

Battery design can be compared against outage priorities, critical loads, and eligible program participation.

Frequently asked questions.

Clear answers before you schedule a free in-home consultation.

Is Axia Solar SMUD?

No. Axia Solar is not SMUD and is not claiming to represent SMUD.

Is the $10,000 incentive guaranteed?

No. SMUD customers may qualify for battery storage incentives up to $10,000 per household when installing eligible home battery storage, but eligibility is not guaranteed and is subject to SMUD program rules, funding, and final approval.

Do I need solar to get a battery?

Battery eligibility and system design depend on current SMUD rules, approved equipment requirements, your home, and whether you already have solar. The consultation can compare adding a battery to existing solar or designing solar + battery together.

Will my bill go to zero?

Not guaranteed. Actual utility savings and bill outcomes depend on usage, rates, system size, equipment, weather, program participation, and utility rules.

What should I have ready?

Please have a recent SMUD bill available, plus any information about existing solar, roof age, shade, and backup power priorities.
