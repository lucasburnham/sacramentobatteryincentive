What SMUD says about SSR

  • SMUD says SSR applies to customers approved to install solar or battery storage on or after March 1, 2022.
  • Residential customers remain on the Time-of-Day 5-8 p.m. rate with SSR as an additional component.
  • Effective June 1, 2026, SMUD lists excess electricity compensation at 9.6 cents/kWh for SSR customers.

Why SSR matters for batteries

  • Battery storage can shift solar energy into evening hours depending on usage and settings.
  • SMUD states battery incentive participation requires the Solar and Storage Rate.
  • Existing NEM customers should review whether incentive participation is worth any rate-status change.

Common questions

What is the SMUD Solar and Storage Rate?

SMUD describes SSR as a rate component for solar, solar and storage, or storage-only customers approved after March 1, 2022.

What is the current excess energy compensation?

SMUD lists 9.6 cents/kWh effective June 1, 2026 for SSR customers.

Do I need SSR for the battery incentive?

SMUD says My Energy Optimizer Partner+ eligibility requires a battery storage unit and participation in SSR.
