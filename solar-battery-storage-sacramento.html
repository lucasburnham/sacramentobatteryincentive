Why pair solar and storage

  • Solar panels produce during the day, while many homes use meaningful energy in the evening.
  • A battery can store solar energy for peak periods or outage backup depending on design and settings.
  • SMUD battery incentives may change the economics compared with a solar-only project.

What gets reviewed

  • Roof exposure, shade, panel layout, electrical panel condition, and battery placement.
  • SMUD bill usage, Time-of-Day patterns, and expected evening load.
  • Backup priorities and which circuits matter most during an outage.

Common questions

Is battery storage only for homes with solar?

No. The visit can review adding solar plus battery together or adding storage to existing solar.

Will a battery eliminate my electric bill?

No savings or bill elimination is guaranteed. Results depend on usage, rate design, equipment, installation, and SMUD rules.

What should I bring?

Bring a recent SMUD bill or online account access so the review can focus on real usage.
