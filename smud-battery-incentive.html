What SMUD currently lists

  • My Energy Optimizer Partner+ is a battery storage incentive for residential customers.
  • SMUD lists eligible battery brands including Tesla, Enphase, Franklin, SolarEdge, Sonnen, and Eguana.
  • The enrollment incentive is based on battery capacity and capped per household.
  • The program requires qualifying battery storage and participation in SMUDs Solar and Storage Rate.

Where homeowners usually need help

  • Confirming whether the property is in SMUD territory.
  • Matching battery equipment to backup priorities, evening usage, and solar design.
  • Understanding the difference between an estimate, final approval, interconnection, and enrollment timing.
