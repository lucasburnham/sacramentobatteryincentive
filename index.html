Common questions before booking

Quick answers for the concerns most likely to slow down a calendar booking.

Is this SMUD?

No. This is an Axia by Qcells consultation campaign. SMUD administers the incentive program and makes final eligibility decisions.

Can I get the quick result without sharing contact info?

Yes. The quick check gives a preliminary result without asking for your name, phone number, or email.

Do I need solar already?

No. The consultation can review adding solar plus battery or adding storage to an existing solar setup, depending on your home and program fit.

What should I have ready?

A recent SMUD bill is the most useful item. It helps compare usage, peak-hour costs, solar sizing, and battery priorities.
