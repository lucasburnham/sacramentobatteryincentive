## Compliance Notes

- Do not imply Axia is SMUD or endorsed by SMUD.
- Do not guarantee incentive approval, savings, bill elimination, tax outcomes, or system performance.
- Use language such as `SMUD currently lists`, `may qualify`, `subject to rules`, and `final approval`.
- Do not add fake LocalBusiness address schema unless a real business address and profile access are confirmed.
