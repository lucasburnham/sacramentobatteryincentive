What is different about Powerwall

  • SMUD lists Tesla as an eligible battery manufacturer.

Common questions

Does SMUD list Tesla Powerwall as eligible?

Yes. SMUD lists Tesla among eligible battery systems, subject to current requirements.

Are ongoing payments available for every battery?

SMUD says ongoing quarterly incentives are currently only available for Tesla batteries.

Is the incentive guaranteed?

No. Incentives are subject to SMUD rules, timing, funding availability, and final approval.
