Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you visit sacramentobatteryincentive.com, contact us, or schedule a free in-home solar and battery consultation. This page is a marketing landing page for Axia by Qcells solar consultation services in the Sacramento area. Axia Solar is not SMUD and does not claim to represent SMUD.

Information you may provide

This website does not currently collect a qualification form directly. You may choose to provide information by calling us or by using the linked Google Calendar appointment-scheduling page. Information submitted through the scheduling page may include your name, email address, telephone number, property address, homeowner status, electric utility, average electric bill, existing solar status, appointment preferences, and notes.

Information collected automatically

When you visit the website, limited technical and advertising-measurement information may be collected automatically, such as page views, referral information, device or browser information, approximate location, and interactions with booking links. We use the OpenAI Ads measurement pixel to understand advertising performance and whether visitors engage with the site.

How information is used

  • To schedule and provide the requested in-home consultation.
  • To contact you about your appointment or solar and battery inquiry.
  • To evaluate potential eligibility for relevant solar or battery programs.
  • To measure and improve advertising and landing-page performance.
  • To prevent fraud, protect the website, and comply with applicable law.

Third-party services

The site links to services operated by third parties, including Google Calendar for appointment scheduling, OpenAI for advertising measurement, the Axia by Qcells website, and the Better Business Bureau. Information you submit to those services is also governed by their respective privacy policies and terms.

Sharing of information

Information may be shared with Axia by Qcells personnel, authorized sales or installation partners, and service providers when reasonably necessary to respond to your inquiry, schedule the consultation, operate the website, measure advertising, or comply with law. We do not state that your information will be sold.

Data retention and security

Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for consultation follow-up, business records, legal compliance, fraud prevention, and advertising measurement. Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used, but no website or electronic transmission can be guaranteed completely secure.

California privacy rights

California residents may have rights under applicable privacy laws, including the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of certain personal information. To make a privacy request, call 385-777-9692. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request.

Children’s privacy

This website and its consultation services are intended for adults and homeowners. They are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly seek personal information from children.

Policy changes

This policy may be updated as the website, advertising tools, or appointment process changes. The effective date at the top will be revised when material updates are made.

Contact

For privacy questions or business inquiries, call 385-777-9692. You may also review the Axia by Qcells Sacramento website.
